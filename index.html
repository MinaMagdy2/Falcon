Welcome to Seawolf diving safaris

Gigantic visibility, fascinating soft corals, mysterious wrecks, a multitude of untouched reefs and good diving conditions all year round have made the Red Sea one of the most popular diving destinations in the world. A liveaboard to the most interesting places of the Red Sea in Egypt is one of the greatest and most exciting adventures in a diver's life. Enjoy the adventure on one of the comfortable ships of the Seawolf fleet.

A Week at Bigdayz is an Experience for Everyone!

Whether snorkeling, diving, canoeing, wrestling, wakeboarding, SUP, yoga or just enjoying the sun on your holiday, it's up to you. Many also just want to read a book and dive into the crystal clear sea from time to time. We offer all this in one package, almost all year round in warm temperatures. On a boat safari, also called a kite cruise, you are far away from the tourist hype and the all-inclusive hotels. You will find absolutely intact reefs, brilliant white beaches and unforgettable sunrises and sunsets. We also offer a wide range of sports at our camps where you travel in small groups of 8-12 people.
